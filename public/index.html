📋 Industry Cases

🏥

Clinic & Salon

Appointment Reminder

No-shows and last-minute cancellations were draining revenue. Staff spent hours making manual reminder calls with inconsistent follow-through.

Solution: Automated WhatsApp & SMS reminders sent 24 h and 2 h before appointments, with one-tap reschedule link — zero manual effort.
Result: 40 % drop in no-shows; front-desk time freed for higher-value tasks.
🧾

Human Resources

Claim Submission & Approval

Paper-based expense claims took weeks to process, caused payroll delays and resulted in lost receipts with no audit trail.

Solution: Mobile claim form → auto-routed to the correct approver tier → status pushed back to staff → payroll system updated automatically.
Result: Claim cycle cut from 2 weeks to 48 hours; full digital audit trail.
🔧

Workshop (After-Service)

Data Collection & KPI Dashboard

Service job cards were paper-based. Management had no real-time view of technician efficiency, customer satisfaction or bay utilisation.

Solution: QR-code after-service form → automatic aggregation → live management dashboard tracking NPS, job cycle time, repeat-fault rate and bay utilisation.
Result: Instant KPI visibility; data-driven scheduling of technician workloads.
⛏️

Mining & Quarry

Fuel & Machinery Maintenance

Manual fuel log books and spreadsheet maintenance schedules led to unplanned machine downtime, inaccurate cost tracking and compliance gaps.

Solution: Digital fuel-log capture linked to each machine → automated preventive-maintenance scheduling → SMS alerts dispatched to operators before due dates.
Result: 30 % reduction in unplanned breakdowns; accurate cost-per-operating-hour reporting.
🚚

Logistics

Order Capture & Round-Robin Assignment

Dispatch coordinators manually matched incoming orders to available drivers via WhatsApp group chats, causing delays and uneven workload distribution.

Solution: Automated order-intake form → round-robin assignment engine → instant driver notification via WhatsApp with job details and delivery address.
Result: Dispatch time cut from ~15 min to under 60 seconds; fair and transparent fleet workload distribution.
💰

Finance

Invoice & Payment Workflow Automation

Finance teams were manually chasing overdue invoices, keying payment receipts into spreadsheets and reconciling accounts at month-end, prone to errors and delays.

Solution: Auto-generated invoices on deal close → scheduled payment reminder sequences → receipt capture via mobile form → auto-reconciliation against outstanding ledger.
Result: DSO (days sales outstanding) reduced by 35 %; month-end closing time cut in half with near-zero manual data entry.
